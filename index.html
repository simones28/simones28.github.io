Simone is a student at Virginia Tech earning her B.S. in Computer Engineering and a minor in Computer Science.

Design for America

Design for America is a national interdisplinary organization that focuses on human-centered design. Simone served as Studio Lead President from August 2015 until January 2017 when she transitioned to Studio Advisor and trained her replacements. While President, Simone oversaw 15 projects, including a project sponsored by Chrsler and several which partnered with the Institute for Creativity, Arts, and Technology at Virginia Tech. She also grew the Virginia Tech branch in membership and professionalism.
